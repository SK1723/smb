Emotion and the Buyer's Journey

There are no good or bad emotions - they all play a role in a customer's relationship with your brand.

Joy increases social sharing. Optimize your tone for joy in your blog posts and social media captions to increase your brand reach.

Sadness fosters empathy between your audience and your brand. Use a sad tone for content that reaches your visitiors that you want to covert into leads.

Fear catalyzes a purchasing decision. Ever seen one of those lightning sales on Amazon? Yeah, that's what we're talking about. Use this when working with a lead that you want to convert.

Anger is proven to solidify a customer's purchasing decision. Use it in your confirmation emails and existing customer communications.

Disgust also helps solidify customers' purhcasing decision. Use it when comparing your brand to competitors
